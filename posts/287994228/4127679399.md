### Introduction

Agent Chat is a built-in AI assistant that helps you manage your todo list through conversation. You can tell it what you want to do in natural language, and it will directly create, modify, or reorganize your tasks for you.

> Chat with AI to manage your tasks!

### How to Use

1. Click the **Agent** button (💬) in the toolbar to open the chat panel.

2. Type your request in the input box. For example:

   - "Help me plan a weekend trip"
   - "Break down this project into smaller tasks"
   - "Mark all the design tasks as done"
   - "Reorganize my tasks by priority"

3. Press **Enter** to send. The AI will respond and update your todo list automatically.

### Features

- **Natural language**: Just describe what you want in plain words — no commands to memorize.
- **Real-time updates**: Watch your todo list change as the AI works — tasks appear and update live.
- **Multi-language**: The AI responds in the same language as your app (English, 中文, 日本語, etc.).
- **Multi-turn conversation**: Ask follow-up questions or refine your request — the AI remembers the context.

### Tips

- Be specific about what you want. "Add 5 sub-tasks for learning React" works better than "help me learn React".
- You can ask the AI to restructure existing tasks, not just create new ones.
- The chat panel can be closed and reopened without losing your conversation.
