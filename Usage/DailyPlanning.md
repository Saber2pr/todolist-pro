### Introduction

Daily Planning helps you track your work time and review daily productivity. It records when you start and stop working each day, calculates your work duration, and shows averages over time.

> Track your daily work hours and build better habits!

### How to Use

1. Open the **Daily Planning** page from the navigation menu.

2. You'll see a table showing your daily work records:

   | Date | Start Time | End Time | Duration |
   |------|-----------|----------|----------|
   | 2025-03-15 | 09:00 | 18:30 | 9h 30m |
   | 2025-03-14 | 09:15 | 18:00 | 8h 45m |

3. At the top, you'll see average statistics:
   - **Avg Last 7 Days** — your recent weekly average
   - **Avg Last 30 Days** — your monthly average
   - **Avg Total** — your overall average

### Setting Work Hours

In **Settings**, you can configure your expected daily work hours (e.g., 9.5 hours). The app will then show:

- **Elapsed time** — how long you've been working today
- **Remaining time** — how much is left in your workday
- **Overtime** — if you've exceeded your target

### Tips

- Work time is recorded automatically based on when you first open and last use the app each day.
- Use the averages to spot trends — are you working longer hours recently?
- Click the **Refresh** button to update the current day's data.
