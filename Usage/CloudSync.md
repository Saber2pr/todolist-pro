### Introduction

Cloud Sync lets you save your todo data to the cloud and access it from any device. Your tasks stay in sync whether you're on your desktop, laptop, or using the web app.

> Access your tasks from anywhere!

### How to Enable

1. Open the app and click **Sign In** to create an account or log in.

2. Once signed in, your data will start syncing to the cloud automatically.

3. Sign in on another device with the same account, and your tasks will be there.

### What Gets Synced

- All your `.todo` files and their contents
- Your app settings and preferences

### How It Works

- Changes are saved to the cloud automatically after each edit (with a short delay to batch rapid edits).
- When you open the app on a new device, your latest data is pulled from the cloud.
- Each file is synced independently, so even large todo collections stay fast.

### Tips

- Make sure you're signed in to see the sync icon in the toolbar.
- Cloud sync works on the **Web App**, **Desktop App**, and **VS Code Extension**.
- Your data is stored securely and is only accessible with your account.
- There is a file size limit of 1 MB per `.todo` file for cloud sync.
