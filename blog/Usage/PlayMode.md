### Introduction

Play Mode displays your tasks one at a time with a typing animation, like a presentation or flashcard experience. It's great for focused review, daily standup, or studying your task list item by item.

> Review your tasks one by one — focus on what matters!

### How to Use

1. Open your todo file and enter **Play Mode** from the toolbar.

2. The first task will appear with a character-by-character typing animation.

3. Press any of the following to move to the next task:
   - **Space**
   - **Enter**
   - **Right Arrow** or **Down Arrow**
   - Or simply **click** anywhere on the screen

4. A progress bar at the top shows how far through the list you are.

### Customization

- **Font Size**: You can adjust the display font size in settings (`playFontSize`) to make text larger or smaller for readability.

### Use Cases

- **Daily review**: Go through today's tasks one by one each morning.
- **Presentations**: Walk through a project plan in meetings.
- **Study mode**: Review learning items like flashcards.
- **Focus**: Avoid getting overwhelmed by a long list — see only one task at a time.
